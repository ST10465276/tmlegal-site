About Us

Brief History:

TM Legal is a non-profit organisation founded by Thinavhuyo Mutsila a LLB graduate with a simple but powerful mission to bridge the justice gap in South Africa for the unprivileged. We believe that everyone, regardless of background, deserves access to fair legal guidance and support. At TM Legal, we are committed to empowering communities through legal knowledge and assistance. Our work is people-focused, compassionate, and driven by the belief that justice should be accessible to all. Whether providing free legal advice, hosting community workshops, or offering opportunities for law students to volunteer, TM Legal strives to make an impactful difference to our communities.

Mission and Vision Statements:

TM Legal provides cost-free, accessible legal advice and guidance to South Africa’s most vulnerable communities, empowering individuals with knowledge of their rights while promoting justice and fairness. We envision a society where everyone has equal access to justice and where law students gain practical, community-focused experience, preparing the next generation of legal professionals to make a meaningful impact in their profession.

Core Values:

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our legal services and people interactions.
  • Justice & Fairness: We believe that every individual deserves justice and equal access to legal resources, despite their background
  • Collaboration: We foster partnership and teamwork with law students, othe legal organisations and the community
  • Education: We provide learning opportunities for young,eager law students learning about their law profession while serving the community
  • Empowerment: We aim to equip disadvantaged individuals with the knowledge and resources to understand and protect their rights.

    • Our Impact:

    TM Legal has made an impactful difference in our community:
  • Over 120 individuals have received free legal advice and guidance on legal issues ranging from family law to employment disputes.
  • 10 law students have gained hands-on experience through our volunteer program, learning practical skills while contributing to real cases.
  • We’ve hosted 3 community workshops, empowering community members with knowledge of their legal rights and resources available to them.
  • Through our outreach, we’ve directly supported families and individuals who might otherwise have had no access to legal assistance.
  • Each person we assist brings us closer to our vision of a society where justice is accessible to all.
