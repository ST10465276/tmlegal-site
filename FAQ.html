FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)


What is Thina Mutsila Legal?

TM Legal is an non-profit organisation founded by Thinavhuyo Mutsila,a LLB graduate

who aimes to provide free legal advice and assistance to underprivileged and disadvantaged members of the community.



Who is eligible for assistance at TM Legal services?

We primarily focus on assisting individuals and communities who are unprivilaged and unable to afford private legal services,

while our focus is on these groups we still aim to provide legal guidance to all to promte fairness.



Can Legal students apply for the volunteering?

Yes. We welcome all interested candidates to volunteer at TM Legal.Particularly, law students are encouraged to apply

as volunteering provides valuable practical experience and an opportunity to contribute to community legal support.



What type of legal matters do you handle?

Our services include:

  • Our organisation focuses on Community education and legal awareness
  • Human rights and constitutional issues



    • Do you provide legal representation in court? No,TM Legal does not represent you in court but we do offer legal advice, referral to pro bono attorneys and mandated organizations where necessary.



    Is all the client information handled confidentially? Absolutely,we handle all client information and discussions with the highest level of confidentiality in accordance with the law and our professional ethics.



    Do you offer paying positions to volunteering legal students ?

    Currently,TM legal being a small organisation does not have the capacity to offer paying positions

    but as we grow, hopefully with more funding will create oppportunites for our upcoming students



    How can I donate to support your mission?

    Donations can be made via:

  • Bank transfer, our banking details are provided under the "Donations" link
  • In-person at our office
    • Every contribution helps us continue to provide legal aid to those in need



    What documentation do I need to provide to receive assistance? All required documents will be discussed and confirmed to the client during the consultation which all depends on your case.



    Do you offer your services only online? No,we offer our services both online and in-person, but preferrably in-person in order to get a better feel of the caseat hand



    Is TM Legal a funded NPO? Currently at the moment,TM Legal is not funded however hopefully in the near future we can obtain it
